FULL SYNOPSIS

I'm Scared of a Lot of Stuff is a supernatural comedy-horror series about a group of teens forced to face their fears when godlike beings turn anxiety, trauma, and insecurity into real, physical monsters. It's funny, messy, and emotional — showing that bravery isn't about being fearless, but about facing what scares you anyway. Each episode puts the cast in a situation drawn directly from their own psychological baggage, with consequences that feel very, very real.
