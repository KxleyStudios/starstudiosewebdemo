LEARNING WITH PIBBY:
ARMAGEDDON

A fanmade continuation of the legendary canceled Pibby trailer. A preschool cartoon character trapped in corrupted worlds must choose: become the hero the universe needs, or let the Darkness take everything.

● In Development Fan Project Non-Profit

FULL SYNOPSIS

⚠️ This is a NON-PROFIT, FANMADE project based on the canceled Learning With Pibby: Apocalypse trailer by CN/Adult Swim. Star Studios has no affiliation with Cartoon Network or Warner Bros.

A preschool cartoon character — Pibby — is stranded alone in a universe being consumed by a glitch-like force called the Darkness. Forced to cross through corrupted cartoon worlds, she must survive the infected versions of characters she once called friendly, gather allies, and make an impossible choice: use the power of the Darkness herself and risk becoming what she's fighting, or find another way to save everything she's ever known. Armageddon expands on the original trailer's lore with new worlds, new characters, and a much bigger story.
