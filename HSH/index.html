HOME SWEET HOME

Three alien misfits hiding on Earth — the armpit of the universe — discover that blending in is a lot harder than it looks, especially when a secret organization is hunting them.

● In Development Sci-Fi · Comedy Original

FULL SYNOPSIS

Socks, Velcra and Patches never belonged on their planets. The three misfits came to Earth — the armpit of the universe — to hide from their authorities, but they soon realize that life on Earth isn't so easy either. When the AAH (Alien Apprehension & Handling) organization discovers their true identities, the trio must navigate the absurdity of human life while staying one step ahead of the agents trying to bring them in. Found family, fish-out-of-water comedy, and actual stakes.
