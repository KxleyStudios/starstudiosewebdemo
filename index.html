We are an indie animation studio focused on original series and fan projects, although we are small... we are doing our best to bring new shows to the table!

OUR PROJECTS

KAZZ AND BIBBLE: WANNABE WIZARDS

Kazz and Bibble are not real wizards. They failed every magic class, dropped out of school, and live in a moldy hill house with nothing to their names but ego and desperation. After accidentally completing a real wizarding job without using magic, they start a business offering magical services they are wildly unqualified for. Each job escalates in scale and danger, revealing cracks in their friendship…

I'M SCARED OF ALOTTA STUFF

I’m Scared of a Lot of Stuff is a supernatural comedy-horror series about a group of teens forced to face their fears when godlike beings turn anxiety, trauma, and insecurity into real monsters. It’s funny, messy, and emotional. Showing that bravery isn’t about being fearless, but about facing what scares you anyway.

LEARNING WITH PIBBY: ARMAGEDDON

A NON-PROFIT, FANMADE Story based off the canceled Learning With Pibby: Apocalypse trailer! The story is about a preschool cartoon character is stranded in corrupted worlds, deciding to save the universe as a hero or risk the Darkness consuming them first.
